    "Apply assertValidTransition in storage methods (server/db-storage.ts):",
    "- updateOrderSubscription: must be coming from 'waiting'",
    "- updateOrderScheduledTime: must be coming from 'subscribed'",
    "- markOrderNotified: must be coming from 'subscribed', 'scheduled', or 'waiting'",
    "- updateOrderStatus: validate the transition generically",
